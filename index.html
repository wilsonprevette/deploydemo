Gibbons

The Gibbon is an ape that is indigenous to the tropical rainforests of Bangladesh, India, China, and India.

There are 18 species of Gibbon. Though they superficially resemble monkeys, they are actually considered smaller/lesser apes because they are smaller than the Great apes.

I enjoy the gibbons' method of transportation as they the fastest and most agile of all tree-dwelling, non-flying mammals. They almost exclusively travel via swinging from tree branch to tree branch and can travel up to 34 mph.
